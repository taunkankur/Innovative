What - SLOB Tool facilitates the cyber Security Learning by Ontology Browsing. This tool will enable you to search different data sources for your learning the security topic of your interest. The data sources include: Youtube videos, Slide Share slides, Research articles at the DBLP repository, as well as a Security textbook
How - In order to learn a topic, you can enter the keyword in the search box or browse the ontology to find the topic and select the term of your interest.
Learning through Muti-modal contents - The SLOB tool will gather relevant learning materials on the topic. If you are mostly interested in learning through video, you will be able to navigate the video search results, and navigate book pages to locate the relevant book pages. This tool allows you to learn through your favorite learning style.
Future features - In the near future, you will be able to gather your learning materials and make a personalized learning repository for yourself.
